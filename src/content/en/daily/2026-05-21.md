## 3. SpaceX IPO Filing Reveals Anthropic Pays xAI $1.25B Per Month for Compute

SpaceX's IPO filing with US regulators disclosed a remarkable agreement: Anthropic has agreed to pay SpaceX's AI subsidiary xAI $1.25 billion per month for cloud computing infrastructure access, extending through May 2029. This means Anthropic will be sending roughly $15 billion per year to a rival AI lab, highlighting how access to compute has become the most critical strategic resource in the AI industry.

> **Awesome AI View:** Anthropic paying a competitor billions for compute reflects the severe imbalance between AI compute supply and demand. Even a top-tier AI lab like Anthropic cannot build sufficient GPU infrastructure in the short term and must rely on SpaceX/xAI data centers. This "coopetition" model is becoming the new normal in the AI industry.
